Summer BBQ Ribs

Description

There's something about the easy-going days of summer that makes you want to pick up your food and eat it with your hands. Case in point: bbq ribs. Sure, you could take your knife and fork to them, but you'd miss the pleasure of pulling the meat off the bone with your teeth and licking every last bit of sauce and spices off your fingers. Scroll through to see which of our best bbq rib recipes will have you likcing your fingers all summer long.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs country-style pork ribs
  • 2 tablespoons of kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 cup of bbq sauce
  1. Place ribs in large pot and cover with water. Stir in kosher salt, garlic powder, and pepper, and bring water to a boil over medium heat. Continue to boil until ribs are tender, 40-45 minutes.
  2. While ribs are boiling, preheat oven to 325 F.
  3. Remove ribs from pot, place in baking dish. Pour bbq sauce over ribs. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until internal temps are 160 F, 1-1.5 hours.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
