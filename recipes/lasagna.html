Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the receipe below, but let's go over the basics.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian Sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and two cloves of arlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, two cans of tomato pastet
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and riccotta cheese
  • Egg
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
